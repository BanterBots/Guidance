What is Guidance?

Guidance is an asymmetrical cooperative multiplayer experience. The goal of the game is for multiple people to work together to guide one player through a deadly maze filed with puzzles and traps as fast as possible.

An Archaeological Dig Gone Wrong

While navigating your way through this newly discovered Ancient Egyptian tomb you are suddenly trapped due to part of the passage way you just came through collapsing! Luckily you have radio contact with the rest of your team who have the map to this dark and dangerous place. But can you get out of the maze in time...
